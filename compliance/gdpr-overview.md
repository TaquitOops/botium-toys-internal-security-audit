# GDPR Compliance Overview

Botium Toys serves customers located in the European Union and processes personal data (PII).

Therefore, the organization is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Current compliance status: **Not compliant**

Key compliance gaps include:
- Insufficient access controls
- Lack of data protection measures
- No formal incident notification procedures

Non-compliance with GDPR may result in significant financial penalties and reputational damage.
