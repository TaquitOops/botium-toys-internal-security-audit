# PCI DSS Compliance Overview

Botium Toys processes online payments and handles payment card information.

As a result, the organization is required to comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS).

Current compliance status: **Not compliant**

Key compliance gaps include:
- Lack of encryption for payment data
- Weak access control mechanisms
- Absence of continuous monitoring

Failure to comply with PCI DSS may result in financial penalties and loss of payment processing capabilities.
